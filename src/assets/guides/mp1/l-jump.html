L Jump

Discovered by: kip, CALFoolio

L jumping is one of the simplest techniques in the game; it's just a standard jump with a button press. It's a very useful technique as it nets you more distance than jumping without using the L button.

How-To

When you begin a jump (including your second jump), hold the L button for just under a quarter of a second (13 frames), then let go of L.

Notes

  • Holding down L resets your momentum gained during an L jump, but it can be very effective to control where you want to land on a platform.
