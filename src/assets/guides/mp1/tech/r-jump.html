R Jump

R jumps are a faster form of jumping that nets greater distance than L jumping, at the expense of losing most control over your trajectory. The Space Jump Boots are needed in order to R jump.

How-To

  1. L jump off of the ground (making sure to let go of the L button), then press R while you're airborne. The R button can be pressed at any time while in the air, but only before your Space Jump.
  2. Now that R has been pressed, your speed will be locked the next time you press L. Press and hold L immediately before your Space Jump, then release the L button.
  3. You'll want to turn sideways during the R jump to maximize your speed. For an optimal turn, hold the joystick somewhere between the up-right and right notch (up-left/left if you're turning left), then holding straight up on the stick when you are fully sideways.
