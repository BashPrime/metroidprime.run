Rapid Fire Missiles

Missiles normally have a slow rate of fire, but by closing the Missile Launcher just after firing a missile, you can fire another one almost immediately and skip the large cooldown.

Rapid fire missiles are useful for dealing large amounts of damage quickly, such as to quick kill some bosses like the Parasite Queen and Sheegoth.

How-To

Make sure the Power Beam is active. With your right hand in a "claw" grip on the controller, hold up on the C Stick, then repeatedly tap Y to rapid fire missiles.

Notes

  • The A button can also be used to close the Missile Launcher. It's a slower method, but it's useful if you're only firing a couple of missiles and don't want to change your controller grip.
