Hard Mode

Hard mode is, per its namesake, a harder game difficulty. It is locked until you beat the game once on Normal mode.

In Hard Mode, the following changes are applied:

  • All weapons deal half as much damage to enemies, except for Bombs, Power Bombs, and Plasma Beam burn damage.
  • Samus takes 1.53x more damage.
