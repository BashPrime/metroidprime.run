Ghetto Jump

Discovered by: Paratroopa

Ghetto jumping (also called slope jumping) gives you extra height when you jump against a sloped surface. Ghetto jumping underwater without the Gravity Suit gives you considerably more height than on land.

How-To

Find a sloped surface (even the back of a Stone Toad works), and perform an L jump directly into the slope; just after your jump begins, either let go of the joystick or hold away from the slope to avoid rubbing against it and losing your jump height.

Notes

  • If you obtain the Gravity Suit, your ghetto jumps will revert to getting as much height as on land.
