L Jump

Discovered by: kip, CALFoolio

L jumping is the most basic form of movement in a Metroid Prime speedrun. L jumping lets you move faster than walking, and lets you jump farther than jumping normally.

How-To

When you begin a jump (including your Space Jump if you have the Space Jump Boots equipped), hold the L button for 13 frames (just under a quarter of a second), then let go of L.

Notes

  • During a normal jump, you decelerate for 13 frames before accelerating. Holding L will prevent you from decelerating, so you will be moving faster by the time you start accelerating. As a result, you jump farther/move faster than a normal jump or walking.
  • You can go even faster by turning sideways during your jump.
