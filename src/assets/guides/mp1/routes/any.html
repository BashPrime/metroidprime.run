Double IS Route

This route has the benefit of being both the fastest and most beginner-friendly route in the game. The Double IS route is modular: all out-of-bounds sections can instead be done with easier inbounds strats without changing the order of collecting items.

  • Frigate
  • Space Jump First dash
  • Elevator to Chozo Ruins
  • Ghetto jump to half-pipe missile, collect Main Plaza energy tank
  • Scan dash over beetle fight trigger, collect Morph Ball
  • Missile Expansion in Watery Hall Access
  • Charge Beam
  • Destroy the missile lock on the Gathering Hall save station door
  • Defeat Incinerator Drone, collect Morph Ball Bombs
  • Missile Expansion in Gathering Hall
  • Defeat Flaahgra, collect Varia Suit
  • Do Sun Tower infinite bomb jump
  • Defeat the Chozo Ghosts in Sunchamber, collect Artifact of Wild
  • Elevator to Magmoor Caverns
  • Artifact of Nature
  • (optional) Collect energy tank in Transport Tunnel A
  • Elevator to Phendrana Drifts
  • Boost Ball
  • Wave Beam, early Artifact of Sun
    1. Defeat sheegoths, collect Wave Beam, collect Artifact of Sun with Early Sun IS (inbounds)
    2. Wave/Sun IS (out of bounds)
  • Elevator to Magmoor Caverns
  • Artifact of Strength
  • Elevator to Tallon Overworld
  • Missile Expansion in Transport Tunnel B
  • Elevator to Chozo Ruins
  • Early Artifact of Lifegiver with underwater ghetto jump
  • Early Power Bomb Expansion with Magma Pool IS
  • Remove IS/lightshow by using Gathering Hall save station
  • Early Ice Beam
    1. Abuse Spider Ball track collision to reach Antechamber without Spider Ball
    2. "Ice Beam Before Flaahgra" out-of-bounds wallcrawl to reach Antechamber (skip Hall of the Elders ghost fight)
  • Jump on Spider Ball track to reach Life Grove
  • Missile Expansion in Life Grove Tunnel
  • X-Ray Visor and Artifact of Chozo
  • Skip ghost fight by R-jumping off stalactite to Life Grove Tunnel entrance
  • Elevator to Chozo Ruins
  • Elevator to Tallon Overworld
  • Missile Expansion in Overgrown Cavern
  • Refill health/ammo at the ship
  • Elevator to Magmoor Caverns
  • Plasma Beam
  • Elevator to Phendrana Drifts
  • Get to Transport Access/far Phendrana by abusing standable collision to skip Spider Ball
  • (optional) Power Bomb Expansion, avoids refill RNG in pirate labs
  • Collect Artifact of Spirit
  • Artifact of Elder
  • Thermal Visor
  • (optional) Defeat Thardus, collect Spider Ball
  • Elevator to Magmoor Caverns
  • Elevator to Phazon Mines (make sure you have a power bomb!)
  • Defeat Cloaked Drone, collect main Power Bomb upgrade
  • Traverse lower mines, use spider ball track or skip it in Metroid Quarantine A
  • (optional) Perform Early Newborn out-of-bounds wallcrawl to obtain Artifact of Newborn early
  • Defeat Omega Pirate, collect Phazon Suit
  • Backtrack to Phazon Mining Tunnel, collect Artifact of Newborn (if not doing Early Newborn)
  • Jump over Elite Control fight trigger to skip Elite Pirate fight
  • Traverse "spiderless shafts" with or without Spider Ball to Elite Research
  • Boost through "invisible" wall to access Elite Research room
  • Defeat Phazon Elite, collect Artifact of Warrior
  • Return to Ore Processing, abuse standable collision to climb and exit the top of the room
  • Elevator to Tallon Overworld
  • Elevator to Chozo Ruins
  • If doing IBBF, perform Ghost Skip to avoid the Chozo Ghost fight
  • Use Plasma Beam bomb slot, collect Artifact of World
  • Elevator to Tallon Overworld
  • Traverse through Overgrown Cavern, Frigate Crash Site again, back to the ship
  • Save/refill at the ship
  • Go to Artifact Temple, collect Artifact of Truth
  • Defeat Meta-Ridley
  • Portal to Impact Crater
  • Defeat Metroid Prime (Exoskeleton)
  • Defeat Metroid Prime (Essence)
